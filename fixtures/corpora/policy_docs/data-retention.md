# Data retention policy

## Customer content

Active customer content is retained while the workspace subscription remains active.

## Deleted workspaces

The current policy deletes workspace content 30 days after a verified deletion request.

## Historical exception

An archived 2024 procedure refers to a 90-day deletion period. It is retained here as superseded history and must not override the current 30-day policy.

## Audit records

Security audit records are retained for 365 days and contain identifiers, actions, and timestamps, but not raw document text.
